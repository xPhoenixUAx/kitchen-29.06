About Us

A Safer First Step Toward Local Kitchen Remodel Help

We are a free aggregator service built to help homeowners get connected with independent local providers for kitchen remodeling needs. We do not perform construction work and we do not replace a homeowner's own due diligence.

Our Story

Created for Homeowners Who Need Direction Before Hiring

Kitchen projects can become confusing quickly. A homeowner may start with a cabinet question and discover that countertops, flooring, electrical outlets, plumbing fixtures, and layout decisions all affect the final result. Our service was created to make that first step easier: collect the project details, understand the category of work, and help route the request toward local providers who may be available for that type of project.

The goal is not to promise a specific outcome or guarantee a contractor. The goal is to reduce friction at the beginning, help homeowners describe the work clearly, and encourage careful verification before any agreement is signed.

What We Are

A Connection Service, Not the Contractor

Providers are independent businesses. Homeowners should compare estimates, check licensing and insurance, review references, and confirm that the provider is appropriate for the requested work.

Focused Intake

We organize project details around practical kitchen remodeling categories so requests are easier to route.

Verification Reminder

We remind homeowners that they are responsible for verifying licensing, insurance, permits, contracts, and work quality.

Local Routing

Requests may be shared with independent local providers or partner networks that handle relevant kitchen work.
