How Homeowner Information Is Handled

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, shared, and protected when you use this free kitchen remodeling aggregator service.

Quick Summary

Aggregator Privacy Basics

We collect information you choose to provide so we can help route your kitchen remodeling request. We may share that information with independent providers or partner networks related to your inquiry.

Important: Providers are independent businesses. Their privacy practices, communications, estimates, and service terms are separate from this website.

1. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to information collected through this website, contact forms, phone links, email links, request forms, and related homeowner inquiry flows operated for . It is intended to explain the role of the site as a free connection service, not as a contractor, remodeler, designer, plumber, electrician, or construction company.

When you communicate directly with an independent provider, sign a provider contract, visit a provider website, or use a third-party platform, that provider or platform may have its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect information that helps identify, understand, and route your kitchen remodeling inquiry. This can include your name, phone number, email address, property address or general service area, project category, requested service, preferred timing, budget range if submitted, uploaded or described photos, project notes, form selections, and communications you send to us.

We may also collect technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, referring page, approximate location derived from device or network information, form activity, date and time of visit, and basic security logs. This information helps the site operate, detect misuse, and understand performance.

3. How We Use Information

Information may be used to respond to your request, understand what type of kitchen remodeling help you are looking for, route your inquiry to independent local providers or partner networks, confirm contact details, communicate with you about your inquiry, maintain records, improve website intake, prevent spam or misuse, and support internal business operations.

We may use your submitted project details to categorize requests such as planning and design, build and installation, surfaces and finishes, utility systems, or full kitchen remodeling coordination.

4. Sharing With Independent Providers

When you submit a request, you authorize us to share relevant information with independent providers, partner networks, or related service-matching resources that may be able to discuss your kitchen project. Shared information may include your contact details, location, project notes, requested services, and timing preferences.

Independent providers may contact you by phone, text, email, or other reasonable methods. They are not employees, agents, or representatives of this website. We do not control their estimates, contracts, licensing, insurance, scheduling, pricing, work quality, warranties, or privacy practices.

5. Communications and Consent

By submitting your information, you agree that we and independent providers or partner networks may contact you about your kitchen remodeling inquiry. Message frequency may vary. Standard message, data, or carrier rates may apply. You are not required to hire any provider who contacts you.

If you no longer want to receive communications, you may follow opt-out instructions in a message where available or contact the provider directly. You may also contact us using the email listed on this site, but providers that already received your information may need to be contacted separately.

6. Cookies, Analytics, and Site Data

The site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site operation, form behavior, security, performance measurement, or analytics if configured. Additional details are provided in the Cookie Policy. No advertising or analytics tracking ID should be treated as active unless it is actually configured on the site.

7. Data Retention

We may retain inquiry records, communications, technical logs, and operational records for as long as reasonably necessary for business purposes, legal compliance, dispute handling, fraud prevention, or service improvement. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and operational need.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no website, email, phone system, database, or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure. You should avoid submitting sensitive information that is not needed for a kitchen remodeling inquiry.

9. Your Choices

You may request that we update, correct, or delete information where legally and operationally practical. Some records may be retained where needed for legitimate business, security, legal, or compliance reasons. You may decline to provide information, but that may limit our ability to route your inquiry.

10. Children

This website is intended for homeowners and adults seeking kitchen remodeling information. It is not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when site functionality, business practices, provider routing, or legal requirements change. Updates become effective when posted on this page.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to . Please include the page or request details relevant to your question so we can review it efficiently.
